Our Story

Growing together with our farmers

How It All Began

The journey of Geetraj Motors started with a simple yet profound vision: to bridge the gap between advanced agricultural technology and the hardworking farmers of Ujjain. Understanding that the backbone of our country lies in its agricultural sector, we set out to provide machinery that doesn't just work, but excels in pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the farm.

A Deep-Rooted Relationship

From our very first sale, our relationship with the farming community has been built on trust and transparency. We don't just sell tractors; we partner with farmers. We listen to their challenges, understand the soil they work with, and recommend solutions that yield real results. This deeply entrenched relationship has been the catalyst for our growth and the reason countless families entrust us with their livelihoods generation after generation.

Growth and Evolution

Over the years, Geetraj Motors has expanded from a modest showroom into a full-fledged dealership equipped with state-of-the-art service bays and a vast inventory of genuine parts. As New Holland introduced more powerful and technologically advanced models, we evolved alongside them—training our staff, upgrading our facilities, and ensuring that our customers always had access to the best in the market.

Today, we stand proudly as an authorized dealer for New Holland Agriculture, not just as a business, but as synonymous with agricultural prosperity in the Ujjain region.

Our Unwavering Commitment

"To provide quality products accompanied by stellar service, ensuring that every seed sown with the help of our machinery brings abundance to the farmer."
